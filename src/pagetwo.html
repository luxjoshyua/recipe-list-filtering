Curry Puffs

Suitable for:

  • All
  • Vegetarian
  • Vegan

Method:

  • 1. Preheat oven
  • 2. Wash all vegetables
  • 3. Dice into 2cm cubes
  • 4. Place vegetables on baking tray
  • 5. Lightly season with olive oil and paprika

  • 8. Serve seasoned vegetables with protein of choice
